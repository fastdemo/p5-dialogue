# welcome to the persona dialogue generator!

inspired by [foone](https://twitter.com/Foone)'s [death generator](https://deathgenerator.com/),
this is your very own velvet room tool for crafting custom persona dialogue boxes.
load up your portraits, write a calling card, and download your creation.

over 3000 portraits from persona 5, persona 5 royal, persona 5 strikers, persona 4,
and persona 4 golden are ready to use.

## how to take your heart (aka use the app)

1. **load your assets.** click "load assets folder" and select the
   `generators/p5/images/portraits/` directory. the app will pre-cache every
   portrait instantly so changing characters, emotions, and costumes is smooth.

2. **pick a character.** choose from 59+ characters using the dropdown.
   the name field auto-fills but you can edit it freely.

3. **choose emotion and costume.** once a character is picked, the available
   emotions and costumes populate automatically. flip through them to find
   the look you want.

4. **pick a box style.** persona 5/royal, strikers/scramble, or dancing in
   starlight — each changes the dialogue frame.

5. **write your dialogue.** type the name and the lines. the text wraps
   automatically inside the box. if it goes past 3 lines, you'll see an
   ellipsis so nothing clips out of frame.

6. **pick a font.** choose from 7 different fonts to match the mood.

7. **download your calling card.** hit the "download image" button and
   your dialogue scene saves as a png. ready to share.

> **a note on spoilers.** the portraits contain major character moments from
